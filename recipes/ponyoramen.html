Quick Ramen

Two kids enjoying some delicious ramen together.

A delicious, hearty meal that is incredibly quick to make, so long as your local market has access to freshly cooked ham slices and boiled eggs. It looks even better beautifully animated by Studio Ghibli in their film, "Ponyo"! Below you will find the recipe to make this incredibly quick dish that anyone can make!

Ingredients

  • 1 package Nissin Chicken Ramen
  • 1 slice cooked ham (cut in half) *use thick one if available
  • 1/2 hard boiled egg
  • Chopped green onion

Instructions

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Place the noodles in a ramen bowl. Pour boiling water over the noodles and cover. Set and start your timer for three minutes
  3. After the first minute, quickly open, shake the noodles to separate, then arrange the ham, egg, and green onion over the noodles. Cover again and wait until the timer beeps.
