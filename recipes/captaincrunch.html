Captain Crunch Casserole

Marshmallow-ey crunchy goodness.

In season 7, episode 23 entitled, "Game of Tones", Fry goes to the past and sees his family. While there, his mother is making his favorite dish, "Captain Crunch Casserole." Here is a sweeter take on the dish, in a style more like a rice krispie treat. Easy to make, and will satisfy your sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 (10 oz.) package marshmallows
  • 6 cups Cap’n Crunch Berries

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, melt butter, stirring to prevent burning, on stovetop over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
  2. Add Cap’n Crunch and stir until well coated. Scoop mixture into buttered dish or pan.
  3. Press into even shape. After cooling, cut into squares and serve.
